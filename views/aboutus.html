Our Story

How It All Started

About us Under the course of Engineering, we stumbled across the subject of Environmental conservation. This website is a result of what we have experienced over the course of this subject. The subject of E-waste and Environmental Management brought us together as a group to create this.

It is a basic yet humble project, and we appreciate your interaction. Any concerns, complaints or appreciations will be acknowledged at below mentioned e-mail addresses.

What started as an academic project has grown into a passionate initiative to spread awareness about the growing e-waste crisis and promote sustainable electronic waste management practices.

E-waste and Environmental Management as a subject

Under this subject we have understood the adversities of wasteful management of e-waste and the losses we incur due to the same, which is a basic component of checklist for any such subject.

What makes it different is its deepened insight on how this subject benefits us not only in terms of environmental conservation but also in an economic sense. It shows us how to create more employment and make use of every bit of resource.

Through our studies, we've realized the critical importance of proper e-waste management and the significant potential for both environmental protection and economic growth through responsible recycling practices.

Our Mission & Vision

Awareness

Step 1

To educate the public about the environmental and health impacts of improper e-waste disposal and the benefits of responsible recycling.

Accessibility

Step 2

To make information about proper e-waste disposal methods and recycling facilities easily accessible to everyone.

Action

Step 3

To inspire individuals and organizations to take concrete actions toward responsible e-waste management through our resources and tools.

Advocacy

Step 4

To advocate for improved e-waste policies and regulations that promote sustainable practices and extended producer responsibility.
