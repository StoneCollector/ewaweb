Reducing Your E-Waste Impact

Extend Device Lifespan

Keep your devices longer by taking proper care, performing regular maintenance, and upgrading components when possible instead of replacing the entire device.

Proper Recycling

Always recycle your electronic devices through certified e-waste recycling centers to ensure hazardous materials are properly handled and valuable resources are recovered.

Donate or Sell

If your devices are still functional, consider donating them to schools, charities, or selling them to extend their useful life before they become waste.
