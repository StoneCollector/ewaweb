Why E-Waste Matters

The Growing E-Waste Crisis

Electronic waste, or e-waste, is the fastest growing waste stream in the world. Each year, millions of tons of electronic devices are discarded, creating a mounting environmental crisis.

When electronics end up in landfills, toxic substances like lead, mercury, and cadmium can leach into soil and water, posing serious health risks to humans and wildlife.

By properly recycling your electronic devices, you can help recover valuable materials, reduce pollution, and minimize the environmental impact of technology.
